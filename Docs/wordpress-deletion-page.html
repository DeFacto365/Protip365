Frequently Asked Questions

How long does account deletion take?

Account deletion is processed immediately when done through the app, or within 48 hours when requested via email.

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No, account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All data is permanently removed.

What happens to my subscription?

Active subscriptions are automatically cancelled when your account is deleted. You won't be charged for future periods.

Can I export my data before deleting?

Yes! Use the "Export Data" feature in the app's Settings before deleting your account.
